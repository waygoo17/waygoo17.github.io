Next-Generation Sequencing Analysis

A template for documenting sequencing projects with experimental context, processing logic, figure-ready summaries, interpretation notes, and curated external references.

Area: NGS Format: Research-note page Audience: Scientific and cross-functional teams

Background

Sequencing projects often need narrative context around sample design, quality control, data processing, and biological interpretation. This page format lets you explain that full chain instead of listing only a short summary.

Workflow Outline

Inputs

Describe sample source, prep method, sequencing platform, and expected output.

Processing

Outline QC, alignment, quantification, variant calling, or downstream statistical analysis.

Interpretation

Capture what the data means biologically and what decisions it supports.
